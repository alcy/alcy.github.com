Jesus says in Matthew 10:8,

“Freely you received, so freely give.”

While everyone has their reasons, I find it uncomfortable that so many Bible translations are copyrighted and cannot be freely or easily reused. Secondly, even though there are great Bible apps and websites, I haven't seen one that focuses on readability and navigation.

But God's word isn't chained. 2 Timothy 2:9

This Bible website tries to address these issues, and uses the World English Bible translation (Protestant Edition https://ebible.org/find/show.php?id=engwebp ).

Inspired by the Tufte CSS project. https://edwardtufte.github.io/tufte-css/
